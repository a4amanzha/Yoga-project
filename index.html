Yoga Poses Guide

Mountain Pose (Tadasana - ताड़ासन)

sah-mah-stee-tee

✅ Improves posture

✅ Calms nervous system

✅ Promotes awareness

How to perform:

Stand with feet together, arms relaxed. Lift your head and straighten your spine.

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana - वृक्षासन)

vrik-shah-suh-nuh

✅ Improves balance

✅ Strengthens legs

✅ Enhances concentration

How to perform:

Stand on one foot, place the other foot on your thigh, and balance.

Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana - वीरभद्रासन)

veer-ah-bah-drah-suh-nuh

✅ Builds stamina

✅ Strengthens arms & legs

✅ Improves endurance

How to perform:

Step one foot forward, bend the knee, stretch arms parallel, and hold the posture.
